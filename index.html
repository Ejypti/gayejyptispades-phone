Start the game by selecting a card, then select 7 more cards. The goal is to view your future with aligning your path. Keep eight cards then once you decide to feel a path of enlightenment on which journey you want to take, lay down with one spade card and the 7 cards of your desired journey. That is the goal of this foresight game. Be careful to keep at least one pottery that can be useful for water. The deck of cards go through once. Your decision must be made before the end of the 11 spade cards. There are 262 cards in the deck. You have 52 draws after your first selected 8 cards. Then a reveal to future ties allies within. Discard one card at a time then draw one card. Once player finds their final cards click both torches. Touch left torch first then the right torch to light. That finalizes the game.
